HOW TO USE

  <input className="input-premium" />
  <h1 className="text-gradient">Title</h1>
  <div className="glass-panel">Panel</div>

ADD DARK MODE:
  className="bg-white dark:bg-neutral-800 transition-colors duration-300"

================================================================================
SPACING PATTERNS
================================================================================

SECTION PADDING:
  Vertical: py-12 (small), py-16 (medium), py-20 (large)
  Horizontal: px-6 (standard)

COMPONENT PADDING:
  Small: p-4 or p-5
  Medium: p-6 or p-8
  Large: p-12

GRID GAPS:
  Tight: gap-3 (12px)
  Standard: gap-6 (24px)
  Loose: gap-12 (48px)

ELEMENT SPACING:
  Between items: gap-3 or gap-4
  After heading: mt-4 or mt-6
  Line height: leading-relaxed (1.75)

================================================================================
ANIMATION TIMINGS
================================================================================

QUICK (Button Hover):
  Duration: duration-250 (250ms)
  Timing: ease-smooth

STANDARD (Theme Change):
  Duration: duration-300 (300ms)
  Timing: ease-smooth

CARD ANIMATION:
  Duration: duration-350 (350ms)
  Timing: ease-smooth

IMAGE ZOOM:
  Duration: duration-700 (700ms)
  Timing: ease-smooth

FLOATING:
  Duration: 6 seconds
  Timing: ease-in-out
  Effect: Vertical drift

PULSING:
  Duration: 2 seconds
  Timing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.6, 1)
  Effect: Glow intensity
